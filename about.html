About

Hi, my name is Tarishi and I am a Sketch Artist . I have been sketching for 4years, and I love the way that pencil sketches can capture the beauty of the world around us. I am particularly interested in drawing people, and I enjoy capturing their unique experessions and features.

Sketch Artist

I believe that pencil sketches are a powerful way to communicate emotions and ideas. I hope that my sketches will inspire others to see the world in a new way.

I am always looking for a new ways to challenge myself and to improve my skills. I am currently working on a series of potraits. I am excited to share my art, I believe that art is powerful tool that can be used to make the world a better place.

For me, sketching is more than just a hobby. It is a way of expressing myself and of seeing the world in a new way, It is a great way to relax and de-stress. When I am sketching, I am able to focus on the present moment and to appreciate the beauty of the little things. I am also able to connect with my inner creativity and to let my imagination run wild.
I am grateful for the opportunity to share my love of pencil sketching with others.
